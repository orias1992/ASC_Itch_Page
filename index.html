The Three-Component Suite

Three executables that work together as a complete, seamless system.

🧠

AI Server — The Brain

Manages your AI's personality, memory, voice synthesis, live tool use, and autonomous cognition. Built on Ollama (local LLM), Whisper (speech recognition), and Piper (neural TTS). This is the core of the entire system.

🎤

AI Client — The Voice Interface

Push-to-talk, audio playback, and a real-time connection to the server. Sits on your desktop, stays out of your way. Lightweight — designed to run on your main streaming PC while the Server runs elsewhere.

📡

Twitch Listener — The Chat Bridge

Connects to your channel via Twitch's EventSub WebSocket API. Feeds subscriptions, raids, bits, chat commands, redeems, and more directly to your AI — no polling, no delays, all in real time.

💡 Recommended: Two-PC Setup

Run the AI Server on a dedicated second PC to avoid GPU contention with your game and OBS. The Client communicates over your local network — just point it at your second PC's IP address. Even a mid-range GPU (8GB+ VRAM) handles it well when not competing with a game encoder.

Voice & Speech

  • 🎙️
    Push-to-TalkHold your configured key (default: Caps Lock) to speak directly to your AI. Whisper transcribes, the LLM processes, Piper speaks back.
  • 🎭
    Emotion-Aware VoiceThe AI analyzes the emotional tone of every response — happy, sad, angry, excited, curious, bored — and adjusts speech speed and expressiveness automatically.
  • 🎲
    Random Wake-UpsConfigurable ambient audio capture. The AI reacts to it unprompted — great for filling silence during gameplay.
  • 🔊
    Audio-Aware PlaybackHeartbeat signals ensure the AI never talks over itself. Clean, professional audio every time.

Autonomous Cognition

  • 📝
    Background Scratchpad ThinkingThe AI silently generates internal notes about the stream, recent conversations, and context at configurable intervals — giving it continuity and awareness.
  • 🗣️
    Auto-SpeakingThe AI spontaneously speaks during idle moments based on its background thoughts. Frequency, idle time, and hourly caps are all tunable.
  • 🔄
    Anti-Echo-Chamber GuardsTrigram Jaccard similarity and difflib sequence matching prevent repetition and loops. If it starts echoing, it backs off automatically.
  • ⏱️
    Adaptive CooldownsExponential backoff when repetitive output is detected. The thinker slows itself down and waits for new activity before generating more.

Personality System

Build a full roster of distinct AI characters and switch between them on the fly.

  • 🎭
    Multiple Named PersonalitiesEach with a unique system prompt, name, description, display color, and icon. Swap between them live.
  • 🧠
    Per-Personality MemoryEach personality remembers its own conversations, completely separate from others.
  • 🌐
    Shared Global MemoryMark important memories as "global" so all personalities can access them across the board.
  • ⚖️
    Importance-Scored Memory StorageA secondary LLM call scores each conversation 0–10 before deciding whether to store it. Casual small talk is filtered out; meaningful moments are kept. Threshold is configurable (default: 5.0/10).
  • 📦
    Bounded, Efficient MemoryPer-personality memory caps, per-prompt injection limits, and token-budget-aware context building keep the LLM prompt lean no matter how long you run it.
  • 🔒
    Cognition IsolationScratchpad thoughts and autonomy markers are never injected into user-facing prompts — internal state never leaks into conversation.

Important Notes & Disclaimers

⚠️ AI Performance Depends on Your Hardware & Model

Intelligence, response quality, and speed are entirely determined by the Ollama model you choose and your hardware — not the companion software. Larger models (13B, 70B) produce smarter responses but need more VRAM. Smaller models (3B–7B) are faster on modest hardware. Response latency is almost entirely a function of GPU/CPU speed and model size. Experimenting with different Ollama models is part of the fun — all models are free to swap at any time.

🖥️ Strongly Recommended: Run the Server on a Dedicated Second PC

Running the AI Server on the same machine as your game and OBS will compete for GPU resources, potentially causing dropped frames, increased latency, or slower AI responses. The AI Client is lightweight and designed for your main PC — it connects to the Server over your local network.

🔒 100% Local & Private

All AI inference, speech recognition, and TTS run entirely on your machine. No cloud APIs. No data sent anywhere except Twitch's own EventSub for channel events. Your conversations, memories, and personalities are stored locally in your AppData folder.
